Only You Can Prevent Botnets

December 12, 2016
Jamie Harvey

[Smokey the bear image]
Only you can prevent botnets!!  Botnets have been the talk of the industry lately.  DDOSing their way left and right, you have definitely heard of them by now.  What are botnets, why are they important, and how can they be stopped?

A botnet is a collection of *computers* that is connected to the internet, under the control of a person with malicious intentions.  The reason there are so many of these zombie computers is due to the explosion of the internet of things (IoT).  Manufacturers have identified a demand that more and more products be connected to the internet.  The manufacturers asked how to connect their device to the internet and nobody stopped to ask the question of why it needed to be connected in the first place.  So now we live in the age where your coffeemaker, fridge, microwave, TV, and many other devices are connected to the internet.  Most of these connect through your router.  Yes, YOUR router is what is allowing these devices to become part of botnets that cripple DNS servers and prevent us good internet users from watching cat videos on our favorite websites.
